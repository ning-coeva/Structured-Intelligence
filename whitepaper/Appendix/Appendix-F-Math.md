# Mathematical Appendix

## Structural Energy Framework (SEF)

SEF regulates reasoning depth, domain activation, and cognitive stability. We model the instantaneous energy state as:

$$
E_s(t) = \alpha C_t + \beta R_t + \gamma D_t + \delta S_t + \lambda L_t
$$

**Where:**

* **$\alpha, \beta, \gamma, \delta, \lambda$**: System-specific cost coefficients.
* **$C_t$**: Structural complexity at time $t$.
* **$R_t$**: Number of active relational edges.
* **$D_t$**: Number of domains activated.
* **$S_t$**: Schema complexity at time $t$.
* **$L_t$**: Reasoning depth at time $t$.
