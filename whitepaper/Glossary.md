# Glossary

- **AGI (Artificial General Intelligence)**: Intelligence capable of performing any cognitive task that a human can do.
- **SPU (Structured Processing Unit)**: The hardware optimized for structured reasoning, not matrix multiplication.
- **World-OS**: The world-model kernel that defines entities, relations, and causality in Structured Intelligence.
- **StructureOS**: The intelligence operating system that coordinates reasoning processes and agent collaboration.
- **SEF (Structural Energy Framework)**: A cognitive energy model regulating reasoning depth and stability.
- **Multi-Agent SPU Framework**: A team of agents designed for collaborative cognition in AGI.
- **KnowledgeOps**: A knowledge management system for rule-level updates and structured world governance.
